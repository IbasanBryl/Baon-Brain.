About Us

BaonBrain was made to help students budget with less pressure.

We designed BaonBrain around real student routines so budgeting feels quick, clear, and easier to keep up every day.

Our mission

Turn weekly allowance tracking into a small habit that builds confidence.

From lunch money to fares and school expenses, students make many small spending decisions every day. BaonBrain keeps those choices visible so users can stay aware of their balance before the week gets stressful.
